2. Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and chicken, cook until no longer pink.
  3. Add vegetables and stir fry for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Pour in soy sauce and stir well.
  5. Cook for another 2 minutes and serve with rice or noodles.
