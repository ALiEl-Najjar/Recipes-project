3. Classic Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and pour 1/4 cup of batter per pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden.
  6. Serve warm with syrup or toppings of choice.
